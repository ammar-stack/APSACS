Academics



The APSACS Academic/Training Department works very closely with teachers for a common objective: to
the quality of teaching and facilitate learning in every way. Our high quality, standardized curriculum has
produced some of the best results in the country. The Academic /Training Department has a robust
Professional Development Programme for teacher training and another support function is to provide
subject specific Term wise CRP (Curriculum Resource Packs) Daily Lesson Plans (DLPs) and Assessment
Packs to teachers.



DLPs allow teachers to focus on lesson implementation; this helps in improving teaching standards by taking
away the daily planning time and effort put in by the teachers for the lesson planning. DLPs have been
structured around the Curriculum Resource Packs (CRPs) subjects and incorporate interactive teaching and
learning approaches. Curriculum Resource Packs comprise of a scheme of studies related to a centralized
book list of private and government publications. CRPs are developed for two terms respectively. The
teachers use these CRPs and DLPs to ensure effective teaching and learning in the classrooms.
